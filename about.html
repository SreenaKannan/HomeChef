Our story

Born from a love of good food

HomeChef started in a tiny kitchen in 2016 with one idea — that everyone deserves a chef-made meal, without the fuss. Today we serve tens of thousands of homes across the country with restaurant-quality dishes prepared by professional chefs and delivered fresh.

We work directly with local farms, obsess over ingredients, and treat every order like it's for our own family.

Our journey

How we got here

2016 — HomeChef opens its first tiny kitchen with two chefs and a dream.
2018 — Reached 10,000 orders and welcomed our first Michelin-trained chef.
2020 — Launched contactless delivery, serving families through the toughest year.
2023 — Expanded to 12 cities and became a certified B-Corp for sustainable sourcing.
2026 — 50,000 customers, 24 chefs, and just getting started.
